# Mesa — Support

Questions, trouble, or feedback? Email **cmagsisi@gmail.com** and we'll help.

Mesa is a private, local baby monitor: one device is the **Camera**, a second is
the **Viewer**, and they connect directly over your own Wi‑Fi. No account, no
cloud.

## Getting started

1. Open Mesa on **two** devices on the **same Wi‑Fi network**.
2. On one device choose **Camera** and place it where it can see the crib, kept
   plugged in.
3. On the other choose **Viewer** and **scan the QR code** shown on the Camera.

*Just have one device right now?* On the setup screen tap **"See a preview"** to
watch a short demo of the live experience.

## Troubleshooting

**"Camera disconnected" / the connection drops**
- Keep both devices on the **same Wi‑Fi**, and keep the **Camera device plugged in
  and on the Mesa screen** (iOS can suspend backgrounded apps).
- Some **hotel and public Wi‑Fi** networks block device‑to‑device connections
  ("client isolation"). A personal/home network or a travel router works best.
- Move the devices closer or reduce Wi‑Fi congestion.

**Pairing won't connect**
- Confirm both devices are on the same network (not one on Wi‑Fi and one on
  cellular).
- Re‑scan the QR code, or use "Enter code instead."

**Permissions**
- Mesa needs **Camera**, **Microphone**, **Local Network**, and **Notifications**.
  If you declined any, enable them in **iOS Settings → Mesa**.

**Alerts or audio not coming through**
- Check the Viewer's volume and that Notifications are allowed for Mesa.
- Sound/motion sensitivity is tuned on the **Camera** device under alert settings.

## Mesa Plus (one‑time purchase)

- Mesa Plus is a **one‑time** unlock (more rooms, full history) — **not a
  subscription**.
- Restore a previous purchase from the Mesa Plus screen → **Restore purchase**.
- Purchases, refunds, and Family Sharing are handled by **Apple**. Refund requests
  go through Apple.

## Please remember

Mesa is a **convenience monitor, not a medical or safety device**, and is not a
substitute for direct adult supervision. It does not detect breathing, vital
signs, or prevent SIDS. **Keep the camera device and all cords at least 3 feet
from the crib and out of your child's reach — cords are a strangulation hazard.**
