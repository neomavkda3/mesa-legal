# Mesa: Photo Booth Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 2026-07-16
**App:** Mesa: Photo Booth ("the App")
**Provider:** Christian Magsisi ("we," "us," "our"), based in Ontario, Canada
**Contact:** cmagsisi@gmail.com

---

## The short version

Mesa: Photo Booth is an AI photo booth used at events. Your photo is sent to an AI
service to create your portrait, and is used only to make and deliver that portrait.
We do not sell your photo, we do not use it to train AI, and we do not track or
advertise to you. There is no account.

Unlike the other Mesa apps, this one uses the cloud to create your portrait, so the
sections below say exactly what is sent where.

---

## 1. Who this policy covers

This covers guests who use a photo booth running the App at an event, and the hosts
who operate it. Events often include children, so section 8 explains how children's
photos are handled.

## 2. What we collect

- **Your photo.** After you tap to begin and agree to the on-screen notice, the camera
  takes one photo so the App can create your AI portrait.
- **A delivery address, only if you choose one.** If you ask for your portrait by email
  or text, you provide that email address or phone number. If you only print it or scan
  a QR code, we collect neither.

We do not ask you to create an account, and we do not collect your name, location,
contacts, or advertising identifiers.

## 3. No sale or sharing

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for advertising. Your
photo and any delivery address are used only for the purposes in this policy. This is
consistent with the CCPA/CPRA.

## 4. No tracking

The App contains no third-party advertising or tracking technology. There is nothing to
change in response to Do Not Track or Global Privacy Control signals, because we do not
track you either way.

## 5. How your photo is processed, and the services we use

To run the booth, the App shares data with the following providers, only as needed to
perform each function. These are the only third parties involved.

| Provider | What it receives | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Apple | App Store, in-app purchase, notifications | Distributes the App and processes purchases |
| Google (Gemini) | Your photo | Generates the AI-styled portrait |
| Supabase | Your finished portrait (temporary hosting/storage) | Creates the download link for QR and delivery |
| Resend | Your email address and the download link | Sends your portrait by email |
| Twilio | Your phone number and the download link | Sends your portrait by text (only if the host enables text delivery) |

Data sent to these services is used only to perform the function above, not for their
own purposes. Your photo is not used to train AI models.

## 6. Diagnostics

If you have enabled "Share With App Developers" in iOS, Apple may share aggregated,
anonymized crash diagnostics with us. This contains no photos and no personal
information. It is off unless you turn it on.

## 7. Purchases

The optional Event Pass is an in-app purchase handled entirely by Apple. Apple
processes the payment; we never receive your card or payment details. Apple's handling
of the transaction is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## 8. Children's privacy

Events often include children, and a guest photo may include a child. A parent or
guardian should agree to the on-screen notice on the child's behalf before the child
uses the booth. We do not knowingly collect information from a child beyond the photo
taken to make their portrait. To have a child's photo removed, contact the event host
or cmagsisi@gmail.com.

## 9. How long we keep it, and security

- Photos are sent over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
- A finished portrait is stored temporarily so it can be delivered; the **download link
  expires after 30 days**. The raw camera capture is not kept, only the finished framed
  portrait.
- The host keeps a copy of finished portraits in the App's on-device gallery on the
  event iPad, so they have the event's photos.
- A delivery address you provide is used to send your portrait and is not used for any
  other purpose.
- To have your photo removed, contact the event host or cmagsisi@gmail.com.

## 10. International processing

The services in section 5 (for example Google, Supabase, Resend, Twilio) may process
your photo or delivery address on servers outside your country, including in the United
States. By using the booth and agreeing to the notice, you understand your photo is
processed by these services to create and deliver your portrait.

## 11. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access or delete the data described
here, under laws such as PIPEDA (Canada), the GDPR (EU/EEA/UK), and the CCPA/CPRA
(California). To make a request, contact cmagsisi@gmail.com. Because we do not require
an account, the fastest way to have a specific photo removed is to contact the event
host or us with the event details.

## 12. Availability by region

The App is not offered in the European Union or European Economic Area at this time. We
may make it available in those regions later.

## 13. Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of the Province of Ontario, Canada.

If this policy changes, we will update the effective date above and post the new version
at this URL.
